WHAT WE DO

Objectives • Goals • Advocacy

Programs that build future architects through learning, service, and leadership.

UAPSA supports architecture students by creating opportunities for academic growth, skills development, community involvement, and professional exposure.

ACADEMIC DEVELOPMENT

Lectures, Seminars, and Research Support

We organize academic programs such as guest lectures, research discussions, and architectural seminars. These activities help students deepen their understanding of architectural theory, design thinking, and professional practice while staying updated on current issues in the field.

  • Guest speakers and special lectures
  • Research guidance and topic development
  • Learning sessions for design subjects

SKILLS TRAINING

Workshops, Drafting Sessions, and Model-Making

UAPSA conducts hands-on workshops that strengthen both technical and creative skills. Members practice drafting, visualization, model-making, and presentation—helping them translate ideas into clear and meaningful architectural outputs.

  • Drafting and rendering practice
  • Model-making and design exploration
  • Portfolio and presentation improvement

COMMUNITY SERVICE

Outreach Programs and Volunteer Activities

We believe architecture should serve people. Through outreach programs, members share their time, compassion, and skills to support schools and communities. These initiatives build empathy, teamwork, and purpose beyond the classroom.

  • Donation and outreach initiatives
  • Community engagement activities
  • Service projects that build character

SHOWCASES

Exhibits, Events, and Competitions

UAPSA encourages members to participate in exhibits, creative showcases, and competitions that highlight student talent and innovation. These events provide opportunities to present work, gain feedback, and build confidence as future professionals.

  • Thesis and design exhibits
  • Student-led events and assemblies
  • Competitions and creative challenges

Advocacy

UAPSA advocates for responsible and socially aware architecture. We encourage students to design with purpose— creating spaces that are sustainable, culturally meaningful, and beneficial to communities. We promote integrity, professionalism, and unity as the foundation of leadership in architectural practice.

Objectives

  • Strengthen architectural skills through workshops and guided practice.
  • Support academic growth through lectures, seminars, and research discussions.
  • Build leadership and teamwork through student-led programs and committees.

Goals

  • Create a strong community of Arki Warriors that supports one another.
  • Promote excellence and professionalism among architecture students.
  • Represent UE–Caloocan in events, seminars, and competitions.
