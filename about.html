ABOUT UAPSA

Our Nature

ArchVision Society is a student-led architectural organization dedicated to nurturing creativity, technical excellence, and professional growth. We conduct design workshops, drafting sessions, model-making activities, and collaborative community projects that allow members to apply their architectural knowledge in meaningful and practical ways. Through structured learning and artistic exploration, we prepare future architects to design spaces that are functional, timeless, and socially impactful.

Vision

  • Service. To uphold the value of service to students and community.
  • Professionalism. To promote excellence in architectural practice.
  • Camaraderie. To build unity among future architects.
  • Global Competence. To understand diverse architectural perspectives worldwide.

Mission

  • To provide opportunities for students to grow culturally and academically.
  • To stimulate awareness of responsibilities as future architects.
  • To formulate programs that enhance architectural knowledge and skills.
  • To foster cooperation among architecture students.
  • To prepare students for professional architectural practice.
  • To represent the institution in competitions and seminars.
