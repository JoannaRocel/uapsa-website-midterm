EVENT HIGHLIGHT

IN-BETWEEN SPACES

January 15, 2026 • MPH 1, 3/F Engineering Building

UAPSA UE–Caloocan proudly shares the success of In-Between Spaces, an Architectural Design 8 lecture series that explored how architecture shapes the spaces where life, work, culture, and history intersect.

Led by Ar. Mikaela Burbano, the event was made possible through the collaboration of the UE Caloocan – CFAD and UAPSA UE–Caloocan. Together, we created a platform for meaningful dialogue and learning.

Keynote speakers and topics:

  • Ar. Jessah Mae Avenida, RMP, EnP – Live, Work, Repeat: Designing Mixed-Use Spaces for Filipino Cities
  • Ar. Miguel Feliciano – Public & Private: An Introduction to Space Syntax
  • Ar. Ysabelle Paula Peñas – Heritage 101 and the Spectrum of Adaptive Reuse
